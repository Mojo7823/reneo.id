# About Reneo Wedding Organizer

## Our Story

Founded in 2012, Reneo Wedding Organizer began with a simple dream: to create unforgettable wedding experiences that reflect each couple's unique love story. What started as a small team of passionate event planners has grown into one of Indonesia's most trusted wedding planning companies.

## Our Mission

We believe that every love story deserves a celebration as unique as the couple themselves. Our mission is to transform your wedding dreams into breathtaking reality while ensuring you enjoy every moment of your journey to "I do."

## What Sets Us Apart

### Personalized Approach
We take the time to understand your vision, preferences, and love story. No two weddings we plan are ever the same because no two couples are the same.

### Attention to Detail
From the color of the napkins to the timing of the first dance, we obsess over every detail so you don't have to.

### Trusted Network
Over 12 years, we've built relationships with the best vendors in the industry, ensuring top-quality services for every aspect of your wedding.

### Stress-Free Experience
Your engagement should be a time of joy, not stress. We handle the logistics, negotiations, and problem-solving so you can focus on what matters most.
